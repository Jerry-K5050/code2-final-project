About Us

Weather Talk is an online platform and forum for real-time weather discussion. it is designed to for users to share the current weather conditions in case of erroneous weather forecast. Based on your current location, we will provide the weather discussion from people near you.

Our Mission

We aspire to provide the most accurate weather information through real-life comments and reports. We hope Weather Talk could become your most trustworthy friend, and we hope that you won't get burned or wet from wrong weather forecast!
